**Goal:** Make boolean toggle switches visually larger and easier to tap on mobile.

**Bootstrap approach:** Bootstrap's `.form-switch` uses `--bs-form-switch-bg` and sizing via font-size or explicit width/height on the input. The idiomatic Bootstrap 5 way to enlarge is:
- Add `style="width: 3em; height: 1.5em;"` on the `<input>` element
- Add `style="min-height: 1.5em;"` on the wrapping `.form-check` div
- Optionally add `class="form-check-input"` with inline style for larger hit target

No custom CSS classes needed — inline styles keep it self-contained with no build step.

**1. In `src/templates/coach/list_detail.php`** — find the boolean rendering block inside the table (lines ~124-130):

Replace:
```php
echo '<div class="form-check form-switch mb-0">'
    . '<input class="form-check-input" type="checkbox" role="switch"'
    . ' name="cells[' . (int)$player['id'] . '][' . (int)$col['id'] . ']"'
    . ' value="1" ' . $checked . '>'
    . '</div>';
```
With:
```php
echo '<div class="form-check form-switch mb-0" style="min-height:1.75em;">'
    . '<input class="form-check-input" type="checkbox" role="switch"'
    . ' style="width:3em;height:1.75em;cursor:pointer;"'
    . ' name="cells[' . (int)$player['id'] . '][' . (int)$col['id'] . ']"'
    . ' value="1" ' . $checked . '>'
    . '</div>';
```
